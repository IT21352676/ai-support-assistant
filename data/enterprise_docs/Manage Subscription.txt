    - This guide will help you set up subscription management within Stripe and will help you customize the functionality of the customer portal.
      - Based on your subscription product, you can navigate to the below location
      - Residential Proxy Subscriptions:
      - Purchase -> Residential Proxies -> Billing & History -> Subscriptions
      - ISP Proxy Subscriptions:
      - Purchase -> ISP Proxies -> Billing & History
    - 
      - Based on the section you are in, you can see the all subscriptions there
    - 
      - Click on the
      - Manage Subscriptions
      - button
    - 
      - Here you can select the plans and cancel as you want.
